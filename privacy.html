Privacy

This site is designed to run without third-party trackers by default.

What we collect

By default, the site stores a minimal, local-only analytics log in your browser (LocalStorage). This data does not leave your device unless you export it manually.

Local-only events No cookies required No external SDKs

Contact messages

The contact form can be configured to open your email client (mailto). If no mailto address is configured, messages are stored locally on your device so nothing is transmitted.

Security

When deployed on a proper host, this package includes recommended security headers (CSP, HSTS, clickjacking protection, MIME sniffing protection). See _headers and vercel.json.
